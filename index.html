****RST

The RST is an app intended to help organize all the sales that take place in my workplace We often find ourselves a little overwhelmed keeping track of all the open orders, This app aims to help that problem

It will Display budgets and amounts shipped month to date. It will automatically calculate the shipped amounts and remaining amounts that need to be shipped.
